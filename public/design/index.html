Design

Every design project, no matter the size, is a joy to work on, whether it’s an entire corporate identity rebrand or an A5 promotional flyer. Each project is an exciting new creative process for both me and my client.

Sylvawood Seeds

Sylvawood Seeds Sylvawood Seeds wanted a product with a look that would stand out from the rest, proudly reflect their eco ethos and look great on the shelf of any garden centre or gift shop. Together, using market research, mood boards and concept development we created a brand that ticked all the boxes and was tailored to suit their target audience. We sourced a print company that could not only deliver the quality of the product that was required but also had the ‘green’ approach that would match the brand.

Cineon Productions

Cineon Productions Cineon Productions, specialists in the creation of immersive extended reality content, had a successful brand with a look and feel they were happy with for several years but they felt everything needed a bit of a refresh as the company was diversifying and expanding. I was asked to create something that utilised their current colour palette and was given a brief that welcomed creativity and a modern look whilst retaining the company’s trusted core values.
