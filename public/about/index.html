Who I am

My name is Lucy and I’m a freelance graphic designer and illustrator. With over 8 years professional experience I’ve produced a wide range of artwork for a variety of print, web, film and exhibition projects, working with a diverse spectrum of clients. I love what I do.

My expertise covers marketing materials, corporate identity (including branding and logo design), report creation, web/UI design, as well as editorial, commercial and private commissions for illustration.

Being both an illustrator and graphic designer I am able to produce complete artworks that are needed for a project. If you are looking for someone to work with you to produce an illustrated infographic or marketing material containing original character work then look no further.

How I work

I’m a creative with a strong belief in process. Here’s what to expect when you work with me.

I’m all ears

This is where our journey begins. Talk to me and tell me everything I need to know... and more. I love to hear about your brand, event, resource, whatever it may be. If you have ideas then great, but if not we can start to get the creativity flowing.

Lets investigate

Lets look at your audience and competition. What works well and has proven impact? What are the trends and how would your project look to a future market?

Venturing out

Now, with that foundation, let me get some ideas on paper and start to explore some options and then talk you through them.

Develop

Now we’re getting somewhere. Let me take your feedback and use that to hone the favourites. Here’s where the detail is added, refinement and a flourish.

...and there you have it

Voilà. You’re happy so I’m happy. Your finished piece in all its glory. Delivered on time and ready to impress your audience.
