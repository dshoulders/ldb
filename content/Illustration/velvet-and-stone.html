Velvet and Stone

The self titled Velvet & Stone album is the third piece Iâ€™ve worked on with the Devon based indie-folk band. They had worked with a photographer and chosen an image for their front cover but wanted an illustrated insert that would compliment their style, the music and the beautiful photograph.

{{< webp src="/images/casestudy/vs-banner" alt="Pencil drawing and finished artwork of a page from the album sleeve" >}}

We discussed possibilities and landed upon the notion of a journey. I wanted to capture a sense of the ethereal to create the perfect relationship between the music and the art.

{{< webp src="/images/casestudy/V&S_illustration_closeup" alt="An illustrated page from the album artwork" >}}

Each illustration takes the viewer on a voyage to the unknown through landscapes loved by the band members, finally leading you down to the shore.

Once the illustrations were complete I compiled the whole insert, capturing their lyrics, and went on to finalise the album artwork in its entirety.
